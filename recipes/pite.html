Pite

Description

A delicious meal from the balkans

Filled bread

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Spinach/Meat/Potatos anything you want inside of it
  • Oil

Steps to make

  • Make the dough
  • Flatten out the dough
  • Fill it with anything you like
  • Form it like a snail and put some oil on it
  • Put it in the oven
